Favicon (optional)

Upload favicon.ico to public_html, then add:

<link rel="icon" href="/favicon.ico" sizes="any">

Simple Email Capture (Formspree)

Drop-in form that emails you signups (replace YOUR_FORM_ID after you create one on formspree.io).

<form action="https://formspree.io/f/YOUR_FORM_ID" method="POST">
  <input type="email" name="email" placeholder="Your email" required>
  <button type="submit">Count Me In</button>
</form>

Cache-Busting (quick)

Force browsers to fetch the latest CSS.

<link rel="stylesheet" href="style.css?v=1.0.0">
